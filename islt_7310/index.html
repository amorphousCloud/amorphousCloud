My name is Reilly. I am a self-taught costumer that has been recreating some of my most favorite characters through cosplay.

I have always found refuge in fictional characters. What began as dressing up for Halloween every year evolved into wearing handmade costumes to primary school as a way to tap into a given character’s traits and incorporate what I admire about them, into my life. As an adult, I lean on the catharsis of cosplay as a respite from the difficulties of navigating adulthood. Cosplay has created a close-knit sense of community that spans continents, where we share crafting wisdom and strategies on how to bring our visions to life.

This portfolio chronicles some of advancements in technique and form I have learned over the last five years, while acting as a springboard for where I have yet to adventure! I have myriad independent projects I have worked on the last few years that I would like to culminate into a portfolio to reflect my ability and growth as a costumer. The only chronicling of my work I have done is disparate photography; as an artist, it is important to display my art!
