beauregard lionett from critical role

Beauregard was the firs tproject I began in 2020 when I returned home from a pandemic-extended deployment. I was in peak physical condition, and knew that this would be my one chance to capitalize on a costume empasizing physical figure in addition to an awesome costume design. This was a fun exercise in learning how to dye fabrics based on their fabric content, as well as how to create a gradient while dyeing. In the completed photos, Beau went through two photographed iterations. The first was with a black shirt and black arm sleeves; the second was with a sewn shirt more accurate to the costume, as well as arm wraps - also more accurate to a monk.
